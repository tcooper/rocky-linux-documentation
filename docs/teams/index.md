## [Infrastructure](infra/index.md)

The Rocky Linux [Infrastructure Team](infra/index.md) dedicates themselves to the management of the infrastructure in the Rocky Enterprise Software Foundation. This group maintains the stability of all components of infrastructure, ensuring the foundation of Rocky Linux and its Special Interest Groups, as well as other projects of the foundation, have a stable platform to use and build upon.

## [Release Engineering (SIG/Core)](rel_eng/index.md)

The Rocky Linux [Release Engineering Team](rel_eng/index.md) (also known as SIG/Core) dedicates themselves to the development, building, management, production, and release of Rocky Linux. This group combines development and infrastructure in a single, cohesive unit of individuals that ultimately make the distribution happen.

## [SIG/Security](security/index.md)

The [Security Special Interest Group (SIG)](security/index.md) focuses on repositories providing extra security-related packages and security-hardened override packages (replacing those from the main distribution) for Rocky Linux and other Enterprise Linux (EL) distributions.

## [Testing](testing/index.md)

The Rocky Linux [Testing Team](testing/index.md) dedicates themselves to the quality and testing Rocky Linux releases.

## [Web and Design](web_design/index.md)

The Rocky Linux [Web and Design Team](web_design/index.md) is responsible for the management of the main Rocky Linux project website, creation of branding materials and support of efforts by all other Rocky Linux teams which put forward the image of the project.
